About Bella-Vista

Our Story

Bella-Vista was started in 2019 by Aarav Mehta.

Aarav grew up enjoying home-cooked Indian meals and wanted to preserve traditional flavors in a modern setting.

Today, Bella-Vista is a place where families and friends come together to enjoy good food and warm hospitality.
